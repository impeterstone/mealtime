    About This Business

    Provided by business

    Specialties

    At J.T. Mchart's we are dedicated to making the world's best pizza.

    Our dough at J.T. McHart's is made from an old sour dough recipe, made daily and hand tossed to each order. We prefer our normal thick curst; however, a lighter thin crust is available. Our Sauce is a mixture of the finest tomatoes available and a mild accent of several herbs and spices. If; however, you prefer a sauce with a little extra spirit, we have prepared for you an extra spicy sauce with a touch of garlic.

    Your pizza is decorated with whole milk mozzarella and Jack cheeses, quality meats and the freshest vegetables available. Every little thing counts, and it's these small perfections that make our pizza the best!

    History

    Established in 1985

    I was a customer first and just loved the pizza, salad bar and atmosphere. I soon learned that James and Tom were selling the business after just  three years. I  liked the fact that it was an independent and not a franchise store. I made an offer, and they accepted. That was 21 years ago Oct 31st 1988.
